Types of Custom Mosaic Designs

There are many different types of custom mosaic designs to choose from, each with its own unique characteristics and advantages. Some popular types of custom mosaic designs include glass mosaics, stone mosaics, and wood mosaics. Glass mosaics are made from small pieces of glass that are arranged to form a larger image or design. They are often used in decorative pieces, such as vases, mirrors, and wall art. Stone mosaics, on the other hand, are made from small pieces of stone, such as marble, granite, or slate, and are often used in architectural features, such as floors, walls, and countertops.

Wood mosaics are made from small pieces of wood that are arranged to form a larger image or design. They are often used in decorative pieces, such as furniture, wall art, and accessories.
