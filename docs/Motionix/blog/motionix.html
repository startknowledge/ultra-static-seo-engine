What is Motionix?

Motionix is an advanced motion capture technology developed to accurately transform real-world human movements into digital data. The Technology Behind Motionix

At its core, Motionix employs a combination of inertial sensors, computer vision, and machine learning techniques. The inertial sensors are often worn on the body, capturing data related to movement, orientation, and acceleration. This data is then processed using sophisticated algorithms that filter and refine the motion information, resulting in an accurate digital representation of the user’s movements.
