Problem: Missing or incorrect h-reflang tags confuse Google and split rankings between languages. Solution: ✅ Always use h-reflang (even for single-language sites). ✅ **Test with Google’s Hreflang Testing Tool. ✅ Auto-generate with Ultra-SEO Engine’s h-reflang generator.

Problem: Machine translations can sound unnatural, hurting user experience & rankings. Solution: ✅ Use DeepL (90% accuracy) + human review for critical pages. ✅ Avoid direct English-to-X translations—rewrite for local SEO. ✅ **Use AI tools like Ultra-SEO Engine’s content optimizer to refine translations.
