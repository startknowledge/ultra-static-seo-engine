❓ Can I fully automate multi-language SEO without human input?

Answer: While AI can handle 80% of tasks, human review is still needed for:

  • Critical pages (homepage, product pages).
  • Cultural nuances (e.g., humor, slang).
  • Brand voice consistency.

❓ How do I choose the right languages for my website?

Answer: Use Google Trends, Ahrefs, or SEMrush to analyze:

  • Search volume in target languages.
  • Competitor rankings in those languages.
  • User demographics (e.g., Spanish in the U.S. vs. Latin America).

❓ What’s the best way to structure a multilingual website?

Answer: Use subdirectories (domain.com/es) or subdomains (es.domain.com) for:

  • Better SEO control (subdirectories preferred).
  • Clear language separation (subdomains for large-scale sites).
